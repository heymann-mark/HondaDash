Lower Torque Rod Replacement

Lower Torque Rod Replacement
1. Loosen the upper torque rod mounting bolt (A).





2. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
3. Remove the splash shield.
4. Loosen the front mount mounting bolt (A).





5. Remove the lower torque rod (A).





6. Install the lower torque rod, then tighten the new lower torque rod mounting bolts in the numbered sequence shown.
7. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
8. Tighten the upper torque rod mounting bolt.





9. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height, and tighten the front mount mounting bolt





10. Install the splash shield.
