Harmonic Balancer - Crankshaft Pulley: Specifications

Crankshaft Pulley
Tighten the bolt to 49 N-m (5.0 kgf-m, 36 lbf-ft) with a torque wrench and 19 mm socket. Do not use an impact wrench.
Tighten the pulley bolt an additional 90°.
