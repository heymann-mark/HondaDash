Antenna Amplifier: Service and Repair

AM/FM Antenna Amplifier Replacement

1. Remove the right side C-pillar trim.




2. 2-door model: Remove the terminal cover (A).




3. Disconnect the connectors (B) from the AM/FM antenna amplifier (C).
4. Remove the bolt and AM/FM antenna amplifier.
5. Install the unit in the reverse order of removal.
