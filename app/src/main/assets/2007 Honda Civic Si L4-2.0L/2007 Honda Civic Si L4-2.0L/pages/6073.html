Radiator Cooling Fan Motor: Testing and Inspection

Fan Motor Test
1. Disconnect the 2P connectors from the radiator fan motor and condenser fan motor.





2. Test the motor by connecting battery power to the terminal No. 2 and ground to the terminal No. 1.
3. If the motor fails to run, or does not run smoothly, replace it.
