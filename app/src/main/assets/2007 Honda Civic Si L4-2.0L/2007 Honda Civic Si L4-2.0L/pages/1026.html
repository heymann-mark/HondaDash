Change Lever Clearance Inspection

Change Lever Clearance Inspection
1. Measure clearance between the change lever (A) and the select lever (B) with a feeler gauge (C) If the clearance is more than the service limit, go to step 2.





2. Measure groove of the change lever.
^ If the groove is not within the standard, replace the change lever.
^ If the groove is within the standard, replace the select lever.
