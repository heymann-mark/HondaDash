Glove Box

Glove Box Removal/Installation

SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations, precautions and procedures before doing repairs or service.

NOTE: Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.




1. Remove the bolts.




2. While holding the glove box (A), release the glove box stop (B) on each side from the dashboard by pushing them in, then remove the glove box.
3. Install the glove box in the reverse order of removal.
