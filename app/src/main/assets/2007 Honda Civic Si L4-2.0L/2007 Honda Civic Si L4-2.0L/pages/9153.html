Windshield Washer Hose: Service and Repair

Washer Tube Replacement

1. Remove the right inner fender.
- 2-door
- 4-door




2. Remove the washer nozzles and clips, then remove the tubes.
3. Install in the reverse order of removal. Take care not to pinch the washer tube. Check the windshield washer operation.
