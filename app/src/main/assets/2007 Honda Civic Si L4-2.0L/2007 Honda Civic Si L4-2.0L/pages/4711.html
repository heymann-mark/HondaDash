Heating / Air Conditioning

DTC indicator 7: HVAC Control Unit Internal Error

NOTE: Check the battery condition and the charging system.

1. Clear the DTC.
2. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and then ON (II).
3. Do the self-diagnostic with the HVAC control unit.
4. Check for DTCs.

Is DTC 7 indicated?

YES - The HVAC control unit is faulty, replace the HVAC control unit.

NO - Intermittent failure, the HVAC control unit is OK at this time.
