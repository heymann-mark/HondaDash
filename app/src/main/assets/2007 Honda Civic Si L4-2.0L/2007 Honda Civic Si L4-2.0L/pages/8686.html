Speedometer Head: Service and Repair

Speedometer Replacement

1. Remove the instrument panel.




2. Remove the three screws from the gauge control module (speedo) (A).
3. Disconnect the 12P connector (B) from the gauge control module (speedo).
4. Install the gauge in the reverse order of removal.
