Automatic Transmission/Transaxle



AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION FLUID TYPE


Honda Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF)

Always use Honda ATF DW-1
(ATF-Z1 has been replaced with ATF DW-1, Honda Service News Nov 2010). Using a non-Honda ATF can affect shift quality.
