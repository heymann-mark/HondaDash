Power Will Not Turn OFF (Without Navigation)

Power will not turn OFF (with navigation)

1. With the ignition switch ON (II), push the power switch OFF or turn the ignition switch OFF to see if the navigation unit turns OFF.

Is the navigation unit OFF?

YES - Operation is normal.

NO - Go to step 2.

2. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
3. Remove the navigation unit. Disconnect navigation unit connector A (17P).




4. Measure the voltage between navigation unit connector A (17P) terminal No. 14 and body ground.

Is there voltage?

YES - Check for short to power on PUR wire.

NO - Replace the navigation unit.
