Upper Torque Rod Replacement

Upper Torque Rod Replacement
1. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
2. Remove the splash shield.
3. Loosen the front mount mounting bolt (A).





4. Remove the upper torque rod (A).





5. Install the upper torque rod, then tighten the new bolts in the numbered sequence shown.
6. Raise the hoist to full height, and tighten the front mount mounting bolt.





7. Install the splash shield.
