Courtesy Lamp: Service and Repair

Ambient Light Replacement

NOTE: The ambient light is built into the moonroof switch.

2-door with moonroof
1. Remove the front individual map lights.




2. Disconnect the moonroof switch 10P connector (A) and map light 3P connector (B).
3. Remove the moonroof switch (C).
4. Install the switch and light in the reverse order of removal.
