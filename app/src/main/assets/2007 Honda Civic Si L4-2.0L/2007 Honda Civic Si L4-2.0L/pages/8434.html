Rear Seat Side Bolster Cover Replacement

Rear Seat Side Bolster Cover Replacement

NOTE: Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.

1. Remove the seat side bolster.




2. Release all the hook strips (A), and fold back the seat side bolster cover (B), and release the cover from the hooks (C).
3. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and to prevent wrinkles when installing a side bolster cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the hook strips and staples.
