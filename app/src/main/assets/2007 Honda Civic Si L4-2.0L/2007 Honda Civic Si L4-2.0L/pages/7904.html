Ambient Temperature Sensor / Switch HVAC: Service and Repair

Outside Air Temperature Sensor Replacement

1. Remove the front bumper.




2. Lift the tab (A) to release the lock, then remove the outside air temperature sensor (B) from the receiver/dryer desiccant bracket [from the A/C condenser].
[ ] : 2-door

3. Install the sensor in the reverse order of removal.
