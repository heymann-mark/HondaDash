Passenger's Dashboard Undercover

Passenger's Dashboard Undercover Removal/Installation

For Some Models

NOTE: Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.




1. Remove the passenger's dashboard undercover (A).
1. Gently pull out the rear edge to detach the clips (B, C).
2. Pull the cover away to release the pins (D) from the holders (E).

2. Install the undercover in the reverse order of removal, and check if the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
